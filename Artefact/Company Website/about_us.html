About Us

Vent and Save have been producing indoor climate control systems for the past 6 months. We are currently working with prototype one but we do have big plans for the next prototype. We are passionate to put money where possible but in peoples pockets which we believe that our systems do to a high standard. We are also passionate about producing a cleaner future, to this we need to cut down in our energy consumption, Vent & Save helps do this. We provide an alternative solution than leaving a windows open for hours or an air conditioning unit on.
