Our Current Product

Vent & Save version 1 is our first prototype of our products range. In version 1, we are currently offering the following sensors shown on the right hand side. Our clients can choose from these sensors from which ones they would like in their system. Our systems can either be posted to the client or can be delivered and installed by Vent & Save installation team. Our systems come in a small size and can be easily portable should the client require to bring the system with them.
