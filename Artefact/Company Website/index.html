Vent & Saves air climate systems are built on the Arduino platform. The system is portable and can be introduced into any indoor room. It can also be used in different climates, it has a high heat tolerance levels.

What we do?

Having a clean air quality is our top priority here at vent & Save. We both design the software and also implement our systems into our clients homes/businesses . Vent & Save aims to be a customizable a possible so that we can meet our clients need and wants.We provide a range of different sensors to our clients such as CO2, Methane and Carbon Vent & Save’s unique software determines when it is right to turn on an indoor fan and open a indoor/outdoor vent which will in turn circulate clean air into the room’s atmosphere.

What is CO2 and how it affects our body

13th March 2020

How is CO2 produceed?

CO2 is commonly produced from the human respiratory system. When we breathe out we are actually breathing gases that our bodies do not want one of those gases is CO2 . CO2 is also produced by the burning of fossil fuels such as peat or coal.

Is CO2 bad for our bodies?

Yes, exposure to CO2 can produce a variety of health effects. These may include headaches, dizziness, restlessness, pins or needles feeling, difficulty breathing, sweating, tiredness, increased heart rate, elevated blood pressure and much more.
